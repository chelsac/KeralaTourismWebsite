Kerala's culture and traditions, coupled with its varied demographics, have made the state one of the most popular tourist destinations in World. Kerala is famous especially for its ecotourism initiatives and beautiful backwaters. Kerala is a popular destination for both domestic as well as foreign tourists. Kerala is well known for its beaches, backwaters in Alappuzha and Kollam, mountain ranges and wildlife sanctuaries.
